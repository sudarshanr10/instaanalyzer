How to Get Your Instagram Data

  1. Open Instagram and go to Settings
  2. Navigate to Accounts Center > Your information and permissions
  3. Select Download your information
  4. Choose Some of your information and select Followers and following
  5. Set format to JSON and request download
  6. Once ready, download and upload the files below
